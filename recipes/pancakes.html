Pancakes

Description

My favourite pancake recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 190ml Milk
  • 155g Self raising flour
  • 50g Caster sugar
  • 1 Heaped teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Put flour, sugar and baking powder in a mixing bowl or large jug for ease of pouring
  2. Mix the dry ingredients then add the egg
  3. Whisk the egg into the mix then slowly add milk whilst continuing to whisk
  4. Continue adding milk until you have a thick batter, make sure there's no dry ingredients stuck to the bottom
  5. leave the batter to stand for 5-10 minutes
  6. Heat your frying pan on a medium/low heat
  7. The batter will have thickened, add more milk if necessary
  8. Pour some batter into the middle of the frying pan to form a roughly 6-8" circle
  9. When the bubbles around the edge of the batter stop closing up, flip the pancake using a turner/spatula
  10. The second side will be done quicker than the first, be sure not to leave it too long
  11. Remove the finished pancake from the pan, repeat from step 8 until no batter remains
