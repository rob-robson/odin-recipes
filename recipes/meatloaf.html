Meatloaf

Description

Tender, juicy meatloaf! Perfect with mashed potato and veggies

Ingredients

Meatloaf

  • 128g Panko Breadcrumbs
  • 1 Onion
  • 750h Minced Beef
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 TSP Worcester Sauce
  • 60ml Tomato Sauce
  • 2 OXO Cubes

Glaze

  • 120ml Tomato Sauce
  • 2 TBSP Cider Vinegar
  • 1 TBSP Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C/350F
  2. Line a loaf tin with greaseproof paper
  3. Mix together all glaze ingredients in a small bowl then set aside for later
  4. Put breadcrumbs in a large mixing bowl
  5. Grate onion over breadcrumbs and mix until all breadcrumbs are wet
  6. Add remaining ingredients and mix well using your hands
  7. Form into a loaf shape, pinch and smooth over any cracks then transfer to loaf tin
  8. Brush generously with glaze
  9. Bake for 45 minutes. Remove from oven and scoop/pour off any excess fat then brush with most of the remaining glaze
  10. Bake for a further 30 minutes. Remove from oven
  11. Leave to stand for 10 minutes before cutting into thick slices
